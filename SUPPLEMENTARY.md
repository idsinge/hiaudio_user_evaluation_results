## Overview

This document provides participant-level supplementary data for the Hi-Audio user evaluation study.
Participants are identified by an anonymous label (**P01–P22**) that corresponds to their entry in the anonymized per-participant document
[`docs/Hi-Audio_Participant_Comments_Anonymized.md`](docs/Hi-Audio_Participant_Comments_Anonymized.md).
All personally identifiable information has been replaced with JotForm Submission IDs.

Scoring procedures:

- **SUS**: Standard formula — odd items: (score − 1), even items: (5 − score); sum × 2.5. Range: 0–100.
- **NASA-TLX (raw)**: Unweighted mean of 6 dimensions. The *Own Performance* dimension is inverted (100 − value) before averaging, so that all dimensions are oriented toward higher = greater workload.
- **SDs**: SUS SD is population SD (÷ n = 22). NASA-TLX SD (summary statistic in README) is sample SD of the 6 dimension means.
